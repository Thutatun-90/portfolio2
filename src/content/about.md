 **Kaung Kin** is an illustrator and visual artist with a foundation in fine art and over two decades of creative practice. His work moves between painting, comics, graphic design, and visual storytelling — blending traditional techniques with narrative-driven imagery.

Alongside his studio work, he has worked as a freelance designer and art educator, collaborating with publishing houses and media agencies. His experience reflects both independent artistic exploration and structured creative production.

## Selected Highlights

- First Solo Exhibition — HANDS Art Gallery (2022)  
- Publisher & Designer — Mee Mee Eaim Publishing House  
- Participated in multiple group exhibitions
